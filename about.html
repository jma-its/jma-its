Our Mission

The Japan Medical Vocational School (日本医療専門学校) was established to train highly qualified professionals in the field of medical informatics. We aim to develop specialists who can effectively utilize information technology to improve healthcare services and contribute to a highly information-oriented society.

Medical Information Department

Our core program, the Department of Medical Information (医療情報学科), focuses on the integration of medical knowledge and advanced IT systems. Students learn to manage electronic medical records, hospital information systems, medical data analysis, and healthcare IT infrastructure.

Why Study With Us?

• Practical, hands-on training with real medical institutions
• Curriculum designed according to current industry needs
• Small class sizes with personalized guidance
• Strong employment record and industry partnerships

Location

Located in Shimonoseki City, Yamaguchi Prefecture, our school offers a peaceful study environment with easy access to major cities. The Kanmon Strait, which connects Honshu and Kyushu, provides a unique and inspiring backdrop for learning.
