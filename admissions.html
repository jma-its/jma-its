Admission to Department of Medical Information (2027 Entry)

We welcome motivated individuals who want to build a career connecting healthcare and advanced information technology.

Capacity: 40 students
Program duration: 2 years (full-time)
Degree awarded: Diploma (専門士)

Eligibility

  • High school graduates or those expected to graduate in March 2027
  • Individuals who have completed 12 years of formal education or equivalent (as recognized by the Minister of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology)
  • International students who meet the above criteria and have sufficient Japanese language proficiency (JLPT N2 or equivalent recommended)

Admission Types

総合型選抜 (Comprehensive Selection / AO)

Evaluation based on motivation, aptitude, and interview. Ideal for those with clear goals in medical IT.

学校推薦型選抜 (School Recommendation)

Recommended by your high school. Focuses on academic record and recommendation letter.

一般選抜 (General Entrance Examination)

Essay (小論文) + Interview. Open to all eligible applicants.

Selection Process

Most examinations include:

  • Essay (小論文 ≈ 800 characters) on topics related to healthcare and IT
  • Personal interview (individual or group)
  • Review of academic transcript and motivation

Required Documents

  • Application form (official form)
  • High school transcript (調査書)
  • Letter of recommendation (for recommendation-based admission)
  • Essay (if required)
  • Health certificate (if applicable)
  • Application fee: ¥20,000

Tuition & Fees (Approximate for 2027)

Please contact the admissions office for the latest fee structure. Typical first-year total is around ¥1,200,000 – ¥1,400,000 (including entrance fee and first-year tuition).
