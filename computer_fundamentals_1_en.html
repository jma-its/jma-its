Course direction

This course begins not with the computer screen, but with the basic principles behind equipment, engineering, and medical machines. Students first learn how materials, forces, motion, energy, electricity, and hospital devices work. Step by step, this foundation leads to a better understanding of computer hardware, medical IT systems, and safe support work in real hospitals.
